About Brain Tumor Detection

This web application is designed to detect brain tumors using machine learning algorithms. It is intended to assist doctors in diagnosing brain tumors by providing accurate and efficient detection results.

Our team of experts in computer vision and machine learning have worked hard to develop a system that can accurately detect brain tumors in medical images. The system uses deep learning techniques to analyze the images and identify potential tumors.

We hope that this application will help doctors in their efforts to diagnose and treat brain tumors, and ultimately improve the quality of life for patients suffering from this devastating disease.
